Interpretation

Variable-importance rankings indicated that shrub-layer attributes, together with temperature, elevation and evapotranspiration, were the strongest correlates of regeneration richness. Ecologically, this pattern is consistent with neighbourhood-scale filtering and local biotic interactions outweighing coarse macro-environmental gradients for early life stages.
