Diagnostics Interpretation

Fold-level diagnostics showed moderate variability in RF performance (R² range: 0.096–0.193; RMSE range: 0.999–1.476) and unstable XGBoost performance (R² range: -0.154 to 0.106; RMSE range: 1.102–1.570), reinforcing that coarse predictors provide only weak predictability of recruitment.
