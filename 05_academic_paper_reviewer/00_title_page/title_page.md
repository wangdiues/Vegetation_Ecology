## Ethics / permits statement

Field sampling was conducted with permission from the Department of Forests and Park Services, Royal Government of Bhutan, under the administrative oversight of the Sarpang Forest Division. No protected or endangered species were collected or harmed. The study involved vegetation plots and non-destructive measurements only; no human participants or animal handling were involved. All activities complied with applicable national regulations and institutional policies. Fieldwork permissions were granted by the Sarpang Forest Division under Permit ID: 2345.
